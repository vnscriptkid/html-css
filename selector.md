## Relational selectors
#### :one: Descendants
```css
.products p {}
```
#### :two: Direct children
```css
.product > p {}
```
#### :three: Sibling immediate after
```css
.product + p {}
```
#### :four: All sibilings come after
```css
.product ~ p {}
```
